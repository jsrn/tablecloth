# Maple Butter

## Ingredients
* 4 tbsp (56g) butter, room temperature
* 1 US cup icing sugar
* 2 tbsp maple syrup

## Method
1. Cream butter with an electric mixer
2. Add icing sugar and maple syrup
3. Beat until smooth

## Notes
* Makes approximately 36 two inch sandwich cookies
