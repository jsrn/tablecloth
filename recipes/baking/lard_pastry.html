Lard Pastry

Ingredients

  • 400g plain flour
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 100g lard
  • 60g granulated sugar (optional)
  • shake of table salt (optional)

Method

  • Sift flour, sugar and salt into a bowl
  • Mix in butter and lard until crumbly
  • Slowly add in cold water, mix until doughy
  • Roll out and use
