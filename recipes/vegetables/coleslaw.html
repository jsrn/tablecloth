Coleslaw

Ingredients

  • Half red cabbage
  • Half green cabbage
  • Julienned carrots
  • Spring onions, long thin slices, 1 bunch
  • 2-3 yoghurt tubs
  • Soya beans
  • 1+ lemon, juiced
  • 2 clove garlic, minced
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Pinch salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil as necessary
  • Mustard as necessary
  • 1 or 2 tsp golden syrup

Method

  • Slice the spring onions and put them in ice water for a bit
  • Mix the sauce
  • Add the vegetables
  • Congrats
