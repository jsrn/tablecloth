<div>
	<h3>Ingredients</h3>

	<ul>
		<li>2 oz Gin</li>
		<li>1 oz Triple Sec</li>
		<li>1 oz Blue Curecao</li>
		<li>2 dashes Bitters</li>
	</ul>
</div>

<div>
	<h3>Method</h3>

	<ul>
		<li>Shake all the ingredients in a shaker with ice and strain into a cocktail glass</li>
		<li>Garnish with cherry</li>
	</ul>
</div>
