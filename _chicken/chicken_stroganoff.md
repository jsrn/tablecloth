## Ingredients

* 250-300g diced chicken
* 300ml sour cream
* 1 tub mushrooms
* 2 onions
* 1 chicken stock cube in 1 mug water
* salt
* pepper
* paprika


## Method

* Slice onions, mushrooms and chicken breast
* Fry onions
* Marinate chicken in salt and pepper
* Fry chicken
* Fry mushrooms
* Add stock
* Add sour cream
* Add more salt, pepper and paprika to taste
