## Ingredients

- 300g chicken
- 1 small tin of sweetcorn (165g drained)
- 2 slices of unsmoked back bacon
- a few cloves of garlic
- greek yoghurt

## Method

- Heat oil
- Add garlic and bacon, fry until nearly done
- Add chicken, heat through
